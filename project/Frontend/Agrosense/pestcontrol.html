Aphids (Green Peach Aphid)
95% Confidence
Small, soft-bodied insects that feed on plant sap. They can cause yellowing, stunted growth, and transmit viral diseases.
Threat Level: High

Treatment Recommendations

Neem Oil Spray
Natural pesticide that disrupts pest feeding and reproduction without harming beneficial insects.
Application:
Mix 2-3 tablespoons per liter of water. Spray during early morning or evening.
Estimated Cost per acre: ₹300-500
Soap Water Solution
Mild insecticidal soap that suffocates soft-bodied insects while being safe for plants.
Application:
1-2 tablespoons liquid soap per liter water. Apply 2-3 times weekly.
Estimated Cost per acre: ₹150-250
Imidacloprid 17.8% SL
Systemic insecticide effective against sucking pests. Provides long-lasting protection.
Application:
0.5-1 ml per liter water. Single application provides 3-4 weeks protection.
Estimated Cost per acre: ₹800-1200
Thiamethoxam 25% WG
Fast-acting insecticide with both contact and systemic action against aphids.
Application:
0.2-0.3g per liter water. Apply when pest population crosses threshold.
Estimated Cost per acre: ₹600-900
Ladybird Beetles Release
Natural predators that consume large quantities of aphids. Environmentally friendly solution.
Application:
Release 5-10 beetles per plant. Best during early morning hours.
Estimated Cost per acre: ₹1000-1500
Beauveria bassiana
Entomopathogenic fungus that naturally infects and kills aphids without chemical residues.
Application:
2-3g per liter water. Apply during humid conditions for best results.
Estimated Cost per acre: ₹400-700
